Ho to do a cheese cake

Description

It's a wonderful, easy, light cheese filled cake

Ingredients

  • 1 (15.25 ounce) package yellow cake mix
  • 24 ounces ricotta cheese
  • ¾ cup white sugar
  • 3 eggs
  • ¼ teaspoon vanilla extract
  • ⅛ cup confectioners' sugar for dusting

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Lightly grease a 9x13-inch pan.
  2. Make yellow cake mix according to package directions. Pour batter into the prepared pan.
  3. Mix together the ricotta cheese, sugar, eggs and vanilla extract and spoon over cake batter.
